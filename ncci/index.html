Conference Preamble

National Conference on Computational Intelligence (NCCI-2017) will bring together leading researchers, engineers and scientists in the domain of interest from around the country by providing a platform to present new advances and research results in the fields of Computational Intelligence.

 

Publications

All the paper submitted through NCCI-2017 will be reviewed and evaluated for its originality, technical content and innovation in ideas and implementation. All selected and presented papers will be published in conference proceeding with ISBN

Extended version of best original papers will be published in UGC indexed journal with prior intimation to the Authors
