Admission Processes

All aspiring candidates are requested to apply online for the Admission. Result awaiting candidates can apply for the course with plus one marks and update the online application forms once the plus two marks are available. Status of Candidates' application and selection to counseling will be informed through mail.

Admission will be based on the score secured in qualifying exam and their performance in counseling.

Selected applicants in the interview will be informed about the reporting date on the day of counselling. Admission formalities of submitting all available original documents and fees for the first year must be completed on or before the reporting date. Otherwise, their admission will be cancelled.
