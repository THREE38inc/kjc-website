BBA (Integrated with CGMA) Admission Processes

Applicants are requested to apply online through the college website once their plus two /equivalent board result is out. Admission to all UG programme will begin immediately after the plus two result of Karnataka PU. Result awaiting candidates can apply for the course with plus one marks and update the online application forms once the plus two marks are available. Only completed application form will be considered for admission.

Every applicant for BBA programme has to go through the counseling (group discussion and personal interview with admission panel) conducted in college. Counseling date will be announced in college website and will be intimated through the respective E-mail ids provided in application form. Admission to BBA program is based on their performance in qualifying exam and the above counseling.
