Research Grant - Shodh Pravartan

Review of Progress & Presentation of Interim Report Process

  • Minor projects shall have one interim review.
  • Major Projects shall have two interim reviews.
  • In the case of a major project, a report of the work done during the period of 12 months / completion of phase has to be submitted at the time of interim review.

Publication

There shall be at least one publication of the findings / outcome of the project acknowledging the college.
